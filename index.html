Solving the Problems of Today

Telehealth has never been more necessary than right now. The COVID-19 pandemic has made it clear that the way in which we practice and conduct medicine is not the most effective system. Furthermore, social distancing practice has been attributed to feelings of isolation, especially among the elderly. Telehealth eliminates the risk of getting more sick, when visiting doctors, not to mention the cost of transportation for patients and staff. Technology has too long been an untapped resource in the medical field. MedConnect’s system is perfectly built to treat, diagnose, and offer check-ins with patients. Our app allows for easy communication and scheduling all in one place, making it the perfect solution to the issue of in-person appointments. Although the need for telehealth has grown over the past few months, MedConnect is an established system. We have been on the market since 2009, building our network and having already worked out any issues that have arisen, like any new business. We have a decade of experience, using constantly evolving technology to our advantage. We are constantly adapting at the same speed of technology, ensuring that our patients are given the most effective and safe care required.
